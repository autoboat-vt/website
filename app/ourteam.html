Software

The Software subteam develops the autonomous navigation systems, computer vision algorithms, and control software that enable our boats to sail independently. We work with Python, C++, ROS (Robot Operating System), and various computer vision frameworks to create an intelligent autopilot software package. We also develop custom groundstation software to control and monitor our boats from the shore, and we maintain and work on the codebase for our website. We are looking for competent full stack software engineers who are interested in working on autonomous systems!

Electronics

The Electronics subteam designs and builds the electrical systems, sensors, and control hardware for our boats. We work with microcontrollers, motor controllers, GPS systems, and various sensors to create reliable electrical infrastructure that powers our autonomous navigation and propulsion systems.

Naval Architecture

The Naval Architecture subteam focuses on hull design, hydrodynamics, and structural engineering. We use CAD software and computational fluid dynamics to optimize our boat designs for performance, stability, and efficiency in various water conditions.

Mechanical

The Mechanical subteam handles the physical systems, including propulsion mechanisms, steering systems, and mechanical components. We design and fabricate custom parts, ensuring our boats are robust and capable of handling the demands of autonomous operation.

Sail

The Sail subteam manages sail design, rigging systems, and sail control mechanisms. We work on optimizing sail shapes, developing automated sail trimming systems, and ensuring our boats can harness wind power effectively for autonomous sailing.

Marketing & Finances

The Marketing & Finances subteam handles team outreach, sponsorship acquisition, budget management, and public relations. We maintain relationships with sponsors, manage team finances, and promote AutoBoat's mission and achievements to the broader community.
