About Us

The Autonomous Boating team at VT designs and manufactures fully autonomous robotic sailboats and high-power electric motor boats for the International Robotic Sailing Regatta and Promoting Electric Propulsion (PEP) competition, respectively. The team is divided into 6 sub-teams: Software, Electronics, Naval Architecture, Mechanical Engineering, Sail, and Marketing/Finances.
