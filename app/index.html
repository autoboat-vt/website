About Us

The SailBOT team at VT designs and manufactures fully autonomous robotic sailboats and high-power electric motor boats for the International Robotic Sailing Regatta and Promoting Electric Propulsion (PEP) competition, respectively. The team is divided into 6 sub-teams: Mechanical Engineering, Electrical/Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Naval Architecture/Ocean Engineering, Sail/Aerospace Engineering, and Public Relations. Each contributes to the design and creation of both our autonomous sailboat and high-power electric motorboat.
