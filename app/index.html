AutoBoat at VT designs and manufactures fully autonomous robotic sailboats and high-power electric motor boats for the International Robotic Sailing Regatta and Promoting Electric Propulsion (PEP) competition, respectively. The team is divided into 6 sub-teams: Software, Electronics, Naval Architecture, Mechanical Engineering, Sail, and Marketing/Finances.

Why Join AutoBoat?

On AutoBoat, you are a member of the team from day 1. You'll gain hands-on experience in robotics, naval architecture, electronics, and software development while working on cutting-edge autonomous boat technology. Join us in pushing the boundaries of marine robotics!
